Why Pilates?

Pilates is not just a sport to look fit. It is for ageing gracefully by staying strong and flexible. Pilates was first invented in the early 20th century as a full-body workout to get a good posture and shape. Ever since then various pilates practices have been developed for different needs, yet all of them have similar goals in common:

  • improved mobility
  • improved strength
  • improved flexibility
  • improved balance
  • improved control over your body

The reformer at first glance might look intimidating, yet you can gain the ultimate control over your bodily strength and flexibility through this machine. A reformer consists of a sliding carriage, adjustable springs and a foot bar and is used for a variety of exercises that train the core muscles as well as the arms, legs and back.
